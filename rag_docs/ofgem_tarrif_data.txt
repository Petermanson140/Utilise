CURRENT PRICE CAP UNIT RATES (England, Scotland and Wales):

Electricity:
- Unit rate: 24.50p per kWh
- Standing charge: 61p per day
- Average annual electricity cost for typical household: £738 per year

Gas:
- Unit rate: 6.24p per kWh  
- Standing charge: 31p per day
- Average annual gas cost for typical household: £1,040 per year

Combined average annual energy bill: £1,738 per year

SAVINGS CALCULATIONS BASED ON OFGEM DATA:

Switching from standard variable tariff to cheapest fixed tariff:
- Average saving: £150-£300 per year depending on usage

Installing smart meter:
- Average electricity saving: 2-3 percent (approximately £15-22 per year)
- Average gas saving: 2.2 percent (approximately £23 per year)

Energy efficiency improvements:
- Loft insulation: saves approximately £310 per year for a detached house
- Cavity wall insulation: saves approximately £210 per year for a detached house
- Smart thermostat: saves approximately £120 per year
- LED lighting throughout: saves approximately £55 per year

Water saving:
- Average water bill in London: £467 per year (Thames Water)
- Installing water efficient showerhead: saves approximately £70 per year
- Fixing a dripping tap: saves approximately £18 per year
- Using dishwasher instead of hand washing: saves approximately £28 per year

SCHEMES AND GRANTS:

Warm Home Discount:
- £150 one-off discount on electricity bill
- Available to households receiving Pension Credit or on low income
- Applied automatically for most eligible households

ECO4 Scheme:
- Free insulation and low carbon heating for eligible households
- Available to households receiving certain benefits
- Also available to households in low energy efficiency homes referred by local council

Great British Insulation Scheme:
- Free insulation for homes with EPC rating D or below
- Household income below £36,000 or in Council Tax bands A-D
- Covers loft insulation cavity wall insulation and solid wall insulation
