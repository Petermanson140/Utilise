<div class="gem-c-heading govuk-!-margin-bottom-6">

  <h1 class="gem-c-heading__text govuk-heading-xl">
    Overview
</h1></div>

      <div data-module="govspeak" class="gem-c-govspeak govuk-govspeak gem-c-govspeak--direction-ltr js-disable-youtube govuk-!-margin-bottom-0">
    
        <div class="call-to-action">
  <p>The Warm Home Discount Scheme has closed. It will reopen in October 2026.
<a href="/the-warm-home-discount-scheme/get-help">Find out what to do if you did not get your discount for winter 2025 to 2026</a>.</p>
</div>

<p>The Warm Home Discount Scheme is a one-off £150 discount off your electricity bill.</p>

<p>If you’re eligible, your electricity supplier will apply the discount to your bill. The money is not paid to you.</p>

<p>You’ll usually get the discount automatically if you’re eligible. You only need to apply if you’re <a href="/the-warm-home-discount-scheme/low-income-scotland">on a low income in Scotland</a> - contact your energy supplier to apply.</p>

<p>You may be able to get the discount on your gas bill instead if your supplier provides you with both gas and electricity and you’re eligible. Contact your supplier to find out.</p>

<div role="note" aria-label="Information" class="application-notice info-notice">
  <p>This guide is also available <a href="/cynllun-gostyngiad-cartrefi-cynnes">in Welsh (Cymraeg)</a>.</p>
</div>

<p>The discount will not affect your <a href="/cold-weather-payment">Cold Weather Payment</a> or <a href="/winter-fuel-payment">Winter Fuel Payment</a>.</p>

<h2 id="eligibility">Eligibility</h2>

<p>There are different ways to qualify for the Warm Home Discount Scheme depending on where you live.</p>

<h3 id="if-you-live-in-england-and-wales">If you live in England and Wales</h3>

<p>You qualify if you either:</p>

<ul>
  <li>get the <a href="/the-warm-home-discount-scheme/guarantee-pension-credit">Guarantee Credit element of Pension Credit</a>
</li>
  <li><a href="/the-warm-home-discount-scheme/low-income-England-Wales">are on a low income</a></li>
</ul>

<h3 id="if-you-live-in-scotland">If you live in Scotland</h3>

<p>You qualify if you either:</p>

<ul>
  <li>get the <a href="/the-warm-home-discount-scheme/guarantee-pension-credit">Guarantee Credit element of Pension Credit</a>
</li>
  <li>
<a href="/the-warm-home-discount-scheme/low-income-scotland">are on a low income in Scotland</a> and meet your energy supplier’s criteria for the scheme</li>
</ul>

<div role="note" aria-label="Information" class="application-notice info-notice">
  <p>The Warm Home Discount scheme is not available in Northern Ireland. <a rel="external" href="https://www.nihe.gov.uk/Housing-Help/Affordable-Warmth-Boiler-Replacement/Affordable-Warmth-Scheme">Find out about the Affordable Warmth scheme</a>.</p>
</div>

<h2 id="pre-pay-or-pay-as-you-go-meters">Pre-pay or pay-as-you-go meters</h2>

<p>You can still qualify for the discount if you use a pre-pay or pay-as-you-go electricity meter.</p>

<p>Your electricity supplier can tell you how you’ll get the discount if you’re eligible, for example a voucher you can use to top up your meter.</p>

<h2 id="park-mobile-homes">Park (mobile) homes</h2>

<p>You apply a different way if you live in a park home.</p>

<p><a rel="external" href="https://charisgrants.com/partners/park-homes/">Apply for the Park Homes Warm Home Discount</a>.</p>
